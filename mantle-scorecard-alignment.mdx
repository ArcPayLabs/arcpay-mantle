ArcPay Mantle is positioned for the **Agentic Economy** track while also supporting the general Mantle scorecard.

The judging spreadsheet uses two scorecards:

- **Part A: Mantle general criteria, 50 points**
- **Part B: track-specific criteria, 50 points**

For ArcPay Mantle, the strongest primary track is Agentic Economy because the product is built around policy-bound autonomous agents, Byreal/RealClaw handoff, x402 paid work, treasury controls, and verifiable execution evidence.

```text
ArcPay Mantle is a policy and payment control plane for autonomous agents: RealClaw/Byreal-style agents can plan treasury work, pay for x402 resources, operate under on-chain budget policy, and submit verifiable Mantle evidence before execution is accepted.
```

## What Not To Claim

ArcPay should not claim an external RealClaw venue trade, DEX fill, or RWA rebalance is complete unless the operator attaches one of:

- Mantle transaction hash
- fulfilled ArcPay order id
- x402 verification result
- signed operator record
- RealClaw venue response

This is important because the scorecard rewards verifiability and punishes black-box claims.
