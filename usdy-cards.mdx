---
title: "USDY Cards"
description: "Bounded spend cards for Mantle agents."
---

USDY cards let an operator give an agent a controlled budget without exposing the full treasury.

## Controls

- create card by slug
- assign agent wallet
- set spend limit
- approve and top up USDY
- freeze card
- activate card
- load card state

## Why It Matters

Agent teams need more than raw transfers. They need spend limits, revocation, and audit records before agents can act autonomously.

USDY cards are the budget layer between operator policy and autonomous execution.
